LIRR AND MNR REPORT


During the work week, the number of people in Manhattan swells to about double the number of people who live there, and the majority (about 81%) of people who travel into Manhattan take mass transit. The LIRR and MNR train networks are not only beneficial, they are absolutely essential to transportation into and out of Manhattan. This report analyzes challenges facing the Long Island Rail Road and Metro-North Railroad, and makes the case that improving these vital transit lines is the most viable, if not the only, way to improve transportation for commuters living along the corridors that these train lines serve.
