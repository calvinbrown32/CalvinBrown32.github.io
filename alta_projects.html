San Joaquin Bicycle Master Plan Update
I was involved in nearly every aspect of this plan. I played a central role in managing the project data and developing the analyses included in the final plan. I used spatial analysis findings to plan targeted field visits where I documented and photographed existing conditions throughout the county. I worked closely with the San Joaquin County Department of Public Works, a sub-consultant, and local active transportation advocacy groups to develop the plan. I attended public outreach events, presented plan findings and recommendations to community groups and members of the public, and created the maps, graphics, and much of the written content in the final plan.
